How the Program Works
  • Drivers earn points for good driving behavior
  • Sponsors control point values and driver participation
  • Products are sourced from public APIs in real time
  • Points can be redeemed at any time
  • All activity is tracked and auditable
Driver Capabilities
  • Secure login and profile management
  • View point balances and transaction history
  • Browse sponsor-specific product catalogs
  • Redeem points and manage purchases
  • Receive automated alerts
Sponsor Capabilities
  • Approve or reject driver applications
  • Add or deduct driver points with justification
  • Manage incentive product catalogs
  • Generate sponsor-specific reports
  • Act on behalf of drivers when needed
Administrator Capabilities
  • Full system oversight
  • User and role management
  • Audit logging and reporting
  • Impersonation for support and diagnostics
Security & Reporting
  • Encrypted credentials and secure authentication
  • Protection against SQL injection
  • Comprehensive audit logging
  • Visually presented, downloadable reports
